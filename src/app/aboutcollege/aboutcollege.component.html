About Us

The University Institute of Technology (UIT), Vellarada, is a distinguished regional centre of the University Institute of Technology, an esteemed initiative by the University of Kerala. Established in 2015, UIT Vellarada proudly carries forward the University’s legacy of academic excellence, innovation, and community empowerment. Located in the serene mountain region of Vellarada, the institution provides a tranquil and inspiring environment for learning and growth. As a proud undertaking of the University of Kerala, UIT Vellarada embodies the core values of quality education and inclusivity, fostering the personal and professional development of its students. UIT was first established in 1995 under the University of Kerala and has expanded into a network of 34 regional centres, each dedicated to delivering accessible, impactful education. UIT Vellarada is a vibrant part of this network, serving as a beacon of knowledge and innovation in the region. Over the past decade, the institution has earned a reputation for delivering top-notch education and empowering students to realize their potential. Beyond academics, UIT Vellarada actively contributes to community development, ensuring its presence has a positive and lasting impact on the region. The institution thrives under the visionary leadership of Dr. Suresh Kumar V R, Principal, and the dynamic academic coordination of Hari V K, Course Coordinator. Together, they guide the institute in achieving its goals of educational excellence and fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity. With a commitment to transforming lives through education, UIT Vellarada ensures students from all walks of life can achieve their aspirations. UIT Vellarada provides high-quality education guided by a comprehensive and innovative curriculum. The serene mountain location offers a peaceful setting for academic and personal growth. Experienced educators ensure personalized attention and high-quality instruction, while modern infrastructure, including well-equipped classrooms and laboratories, creates an ideal environment for learning. The institution actively engages with local communities, contributing to regional development and empowerment. UIT Vellarada is more than an academic institution—it is a transformative force, preparing students to face the challenges of the modern world while instilling a sense of responsibility and purpose in them. As a proud part of the University of Kerala’s prestigious network, UIT Vellarada stands as a symbol of progress, excellence, and hope for the future. With its motto, "Educate · Engage · Empower," the institute is committed to nurturing capable leaders and changemakers who will make a difference in their fields and communities.
